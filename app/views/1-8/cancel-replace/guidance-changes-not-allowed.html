You may need to ask for a replacement certificate

If the importing country does not allow you to make handwritten changes, you may need to ask for a replacement.

APHA will not issue a replacement certificate if the original certificate:

  • has been lost or damaged
  • was not issued by the certifying officer

You do not need a replacement certificate if the consignment has not yet left the UK.
