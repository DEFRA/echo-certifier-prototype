Can you replace this certificate?

If you still have the certificate, contact the importing country. They might accept an amended certified copy of the certificate, and you will not need to request a replacement.

You can request to replace this certificate if it has been certified and:

  • the certificate is lost in transit and the consignment has left the UK
  • you need to change the information in the certificate and it must be fully typed

You cannot request a replacement if:

  • APHA have not processed the certificate or dispatched it
  • the certificate is lost in the post on its way to the certifier
  • the certificate is certified, can be amended by hand and has not left the UK

You will need to attach a written request for a replacement certificate from the importing country. Add it as a supporting document at the end of this application.
