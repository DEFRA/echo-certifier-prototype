Register as an Official Veterinarian or authorised veterinarian inspector

Use this service to register as an Official Veterinarian (OV) and work on Export Health Certificate (EHC) cases online.

You will need to use a Government Gateway account to register for this service.

How to register

Use or create your own Government Gateway account to register with this service, and other services provided by Defra and related agencies and public bodies.

Having your own set of sign in details (a Government Gateway user ID and password) will save you time.

Defra will hold basic information about you so that you do not have to re-enter information each time you want to use this service.

Before you start

To register for this online service you will be asked to:

  1. Enter your Government Gateway user ID and password if you already have an account. If not, you will be guided through the process of creating one.
  2. Tell us some information about you and how you want to use our online services.

You will need:

  • your email address, phone number and postal address
  • your Government Gateway user ID and password (if you already have an account, if not you will be guided to create one)

If you are registering on behalf of a business or organisation, you will also need:

  • the main contact details for your business or organisation (for example, head office contact details)
  • the Companies House number if you are registering on behalf of a limited company
